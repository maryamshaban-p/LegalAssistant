
Title: Copyright Law
Category: IP-Law
Description: Protects authors’ works including books, music, software, and digital content.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Copying software without permission.
- Registering literary works or music compositions.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 7: Copyright lasts life + 50 years.
- Article 15: Registration provides proof in disputes.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Fair use allowed for education or private use.

Title: Trademark Law
Category: IP-Law
Description: Protects logos, brands, and commercial identifiers.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Registering a company logo.
- Disputing unauthorized use of a trademark.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 5: Trademarks must be distinctive.
- Article 12: Exclusive rights granted for registered trademarks.
- Article 18: Protection lasts 10 years, renewable.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Generic or descriptive terms cannot be trademarked.

Title: Patent Law
Category: IP-Law
Description: Protects inventions and technical innovations.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Filing patent applications for inventions.
- Enforcement against infringement.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 30: Patent protection lasts 20 years.
- Article 35: Must be new, inventive, and industrially applicable.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Natural discoveries and methods of treatment are excluded.

Title: Industrial Design Law
Category: IP-Law
Description: Protects aesthetic designs of products.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Protecting the visual design of furniture or electronics.
- Enforcement against copying of designs.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 40: Protection lasts 15 years.
- Article 42: Registration required for enforcement.

Title: Trade Secrets Law
Category: IP-Law
Description: Protects confidential business information.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Unauthorized disclosure of formulas or business strategies.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 50: Misappropriation is punishable by civil or criminal penalties.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Information lawfully obtained or publicly known is not protected.

Title: Domain Name and Digital IP
Category: IP-Law
Description: Protects domain names and online brand presence.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Cybersquatting disputes.
- Online trademark enforcement.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 60: Rights extend to domain names identical to trademarks.

Title: Licensing and Technology Transfer
Category: IP-Law
Description: Rules for licensing IP and transferring technology.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Software licensing agreements.
- Technology transfer agreements between companies.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 70: Licensing agreements must respect IP rights.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Unauthorized sublicensing constitutes infringement.
