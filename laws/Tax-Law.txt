
Title: Income Tax Law
Category: Tax-Law
Description: Governs taxation on individuals and corporations. Covers income calculation, deductions, filing, and penalties.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Calculating personal income tax for salaried employees.
- Corporate tax rates for small and large enterprises.
- Filing deadlines and penalties for late submission.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 1: All Egyptian-source income is taxable.
- Article 3: Tax rates depend on annual income brackets.
- Article 5: Corporations must file tax returns annually.
- Article 8: Tax deductions available for education, medical expenses, and charitable contributions.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Charitable organizations may be partially or fully exempt.
- Tax incentives exist for investments in specific economic zones.

Title: Value Added Tax (VAT)
Category: Tax-Law
Description: VAT applies to goods and services. Defines registration, collection, exemptions, and penalties.
Examples / Use Cases:
- VAT registration for small business owners.
- Reporting VAT for e-commerce sales.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 10: Standard VAT rate is 14%.
- Article 12: Exemptions for basic food items, healthcare, and education.
- Article 15: Tax invoices must be issued for all taxable transactions.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Exported goods are zero-rated.
- Non-resident suppliers providing services to Egypt must register for VAT.

Title: Corporate Tax Incentives
Category: Tax-Law
Description: Reduced tax rates or exemptions for specific businesses.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Special rates for tech startups.
- Tax-free zones for industrial projects.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 20: Incentives apply for investments in renewable energy, manufacturing, and export-oriented projects.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Incentives require official registration and approval.

Title: Property Tax
Category: Tax-Law
Description: Taxation on real estate and land ownership.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Annual property tax for residential and commercial properties.
- Tax relief for agricultural land.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 25: Tax calculated on assessed property value.
- Article 28: Municipal tax obligations.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Properties used for charitable purposes may be exempt.

Title: Customs Duties and Excise
Category: Tax-Law
Description: Taxes on imported goods and specific products.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Importing machinery and paying customs duties.
- Excise taxes on tobacco and alcohol.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 35: Customs duties calculated based on CIF value.
- Article 40: Excise taxes applied to luxury goods.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Exemptions for free trade agreements and bonded zones.

Title: Tax Filing and Penalties
Category: Tax-Law
Description: Procedures for tax submission and consequences for non-compliance.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Filing annual returns electronically.
- Penalties for late payment or under-reporting.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 50: Returns must be filed within specified deadlines.
- Article 52: Late filing incurs fines and interest.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Extensions may be granted under special circumstances.

Title: Tax Treaties and International Taxation
Category: Tax-Law
Description: Agreements to avoid double taxation and regulate cross-border taxes.
Examples / Use Cases:
- Tax obligations for foreign businesses operating in Egypt.
- Treaties with other countries to prevent double taxation.
Key Articles / Rules:
- Article 60: Tax treaty provisions override domestic laws if agreed internationally.
Notes / Exceptions:
- Domestic anti-avoidance rules still apply.
